About Founder

Alec Soth is an American photographer,
based in Minneapolis. Soth makes "large-
scale American projects" featuring the
midwestern United States. New York Times
art critic Hilarie M. Sheets wrote that he has
made a "photographic career out of finding
chemistry with strangers" and photographs
"loners and dreamers".
